CANoe CV2x Day1 Scenarios
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

This sample configuration contains different scenarios and can be used
in simulated or real bus mode. Deactivate the node 'HV_Simulator' if the 
measurement is performed on the real bus and the tested vehicle and its 
Car2x communication behavior are real. 

Each scenario can be started at the current position either relative or on 
a specified route. Specify the route that the tested vehicle is driving on 
by giving the filename and the name of the route.

When the node 'HV_Simulator' is active, the ego vehicle is simulated 
driving on an example route with adaptable speed.

This sample configuration contains the following scenarios:
AVW - Abnormal Vehicle Warning
BCW (LCW) - Blind Spot (Lane Change) Warning
CLW - Control Loss Warning
DNPW - Do Not Pass Warning
EBW - Emergency Brake Warning
EVW - Emergency Vehicle Warning
FCW - Forward Collision Warning
HLW - Hazardous Location Warning
ICW - Intersection Collision Warning
IVS - In-Vehicle Signage
LTA - Left Turn Assist
SLW - Speed Limit Warning
TJW - Traffic Jam Warning
VRUCW - Vulnerable Road User Collision Warning

The scenarios are in defined in T/CSAE 53-2017, Ch. 5.2
