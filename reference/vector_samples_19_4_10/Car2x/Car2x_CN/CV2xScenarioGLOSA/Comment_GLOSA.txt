GLOSA - Green Light Optimal Speed Advisory scenario.

According to T/CSAE 53-2017, GLOSA Scenario Ch. 5.2.14

(a) HV vehicle approaches at V2I-equipped intersection

The road side unit (RSU) broadcasts the topology an traffic lights signal 
state of the road intersection. The HV vehicle drives from a distance
towards the intersection. The GLOSA application in the HV vehicles
calculates the minimum an maximum speed advised to cross the intersection
without stopping. The calculation use the position data, speed and 
intersection traffic lights data for this purpose.
