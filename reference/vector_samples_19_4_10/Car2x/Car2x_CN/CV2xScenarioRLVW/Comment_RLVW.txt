RLVW - Red Light Violation Warning scenario.

According to T/CSAE 53-2017, RLVW Scenario Ch. 5.2.11

(a) The HV vehicle crosses the intersection on red traffic light

The HV vehicle is driving toward the traffic signal controlled intersection and
arrives at the stop line at the time point when the signal for this line is in
red light state.

The RLVW application warns the HV vehicle driver upon analyzing the received
intersection data.
